Client Management

Add New Client

You can choose an existing client or add a new one by typing above and pressing enter.

Clients can be described by IP addresses (both IPv4 and IPv6 supported), IP subnets (CIDR notation, e.g., 192.168.2.0/24), MAC addresses (e.g., 12:34:56:78:9A:BC), hostnames (e.g., localhost), or interfaces they are connected to (prefixed with a colon, e.g., :eth0).
